The Best films According to John

My Favourite Films

Spirited Away

A thrilling fantasy anime film steeped in Japanese folk lore.
Very entertaining, family friendly and action packed.

Neverending Story

Another family friendly anime film that takes you away into a fantasy land where not much else seems to matter for the duration of the film.

Kill Bill

In stark contrast to the previous 2 films Kill Bill is quite gory and sensational with lots of gratuitous violence but in a typically Quentin Tarrantino style he's managed to make it quite comical and surreal.

Boon Dock Saints

A funny look at gangland in Glasgow with some hillarious yet bloodthirsty moments. Starring Billy Connolly at his comedic best.
